About Space Club

Pushing the boundaries of student aerospace innovation since 2015

Our Mission

To inspire and educate the next generation of aerospace engineers through hands-on rocketry and satellite projects while fostering innovation and collaboration.

Our Vision

To become a leading student aerospace organization recognized for groundbreaking research and successful space missions.

Our Values

Innovation, Collaboration, Excellence, and Passion for space exploration and technology.

Who We Are

Space Club RIT is a multidisciplinary team of passionate students dedicated to aerospace engineering and space technology. Founded in 2017, we bring together students from various disciplines including engineering, computer science, physics, and design to work on cutting-edge space projects.

Our team consists of over 50 active members working across six specialized subteams, each focusing on different aspects of aerospace engineering.

What We Do

Rocket Development

Design, build, and launch high-power rockets reaching altitudes of 30,000+ feet with advanced recovery systems.

Satellite Projects

Develop CubeSat satellites for scientific research and technology demonstration missions.

Avionics Systems

Create custom flight computers, telemetry systems, and ground station software.

Propulsion Research

Design and test hybrid and liquid rocket engines in our propulsion lab.

Education & Outreach

Host workshops, mentor local students, and organize space-related events.

Industry Collaboration

Partner with aerospace companies and research institutions on advanced projects.
